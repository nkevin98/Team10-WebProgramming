Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of 1/2" cubed chicken tenderloins (or thighs)
  • 2 tbsp red chili pepper (up to your taste really...)
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric
  • 1/3 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1/3 tbsp ground fenugreek
  • 1/3 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp grated garlic
  • 1 tbsp red food coloring (optional but recommended for that beautiful color!)
  • 1/2 cup corn starch

Instructions


  1. Mix all ingredients excluding the corn starch in a bowl
  2. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes
  3. While your chicken marinates, heat a pan up with vegetable oil to about medium-high
  4. Place chicken in pan and turn frequently to evenly cook chicken
  5. Serve on plate with paper towel and enjoy
